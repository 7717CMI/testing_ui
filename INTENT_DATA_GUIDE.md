## Understanding Intent Scores

### Score Ranges
- **0-69**: Low intent (early awareness)
- **70-84**: Medium intent (active research)
- **85-100**: High intent (late-stage buying)

### Buying Stages
- **Early Stage** (Score 0-69): Initial research, problem awareness
- **Mid Stage** (Score 70-84): Solution evaluation, comparing options
- **Late Stage** (Score 85-100): Vendor selection, ready to buy

### Trend Indicators
- **üî∫ Up**: Research activity increasing (positive signal)
- **‚ûñ Stable**: Consistent research activity
- **üîª Down**: Research activity decreasing
